About Julia Schiff

I am a journalist, writer, and artist based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I cover arts, culture, and social issues, contributing to a range of publications including Marketplace, Minneapolis St. Paul Magazine, The Current, the Pioneer Press, and the Star Tribune.

My work combines research, storytelling, and illustration to bring clarity and creativity to the subjects I explore.

In my free time, I enjoy printmaking, swimming in lakes, baking, and maintaining a steadfastly sub-mediocre basketball game.

I’m also conducting an ongoing, multi-year investigation into the best pepperoni pizza in the Twin Cities. If you have any strong leads (or strong opinions), please don’t hesitate to email me at juliaschiff2@gmail.com.
